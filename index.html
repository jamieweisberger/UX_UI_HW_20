Government Agency Website Redesign

PROJECT SUMMARY: I created a redesign of the Department of Energy to enhance easy of use and accessibility to allow users to search more clearly for the information they are looking for without becoming overwhelmed and frustrated with the content provided. In this research project, I planned and conducted 3 interviews with current users, completed a heuristic analysis to gain insights over the current usability issues, and conducted usability testing to ensure my designs were taking user consideration into account.

1. Conducted a heuritisic analysis to identify usability issues

2. Performed Usability testing to idenfify the main pain points users are facing

3. Created proto personas to determine the ideal user for the Department of Energy website.

Problem Statement

The Problem: The Department of Energy Website is overloaded with articles and information thus causing frustration among users looking for specific information. The Solution: I have created a redesign of the Department of Energy to allow users to search more clearly for the information they are looking for, while not being overloaded with information that is not relevant to them.
